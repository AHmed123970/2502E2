# Privacy Policy for TraderPro

**Last Updated: November 1, 2025**

## Introduction

TraderPro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

TraderPro is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

### Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:
- Portfolio holdings (stocks you own)
- Transaction history (your buy/sell trades)
- Watchlist (stocks you're tracking)
- Cash balance

This data is stored using your device's local storage and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device information
- Usage analytics
- Advertising identifiers
- Any other personal data

## Third-Party Services

TraderPro does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The app operates entirely offline using simulated market data.

## Market Data

All stock market data displayed in the app is simulated and generated locally for educational and demonstration purposes. No real market data APIs are used, and no data is transmitted over the internet.

## Children's Privacy

TraderPro is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- Your data is protected by your device's security measures
- We recommend using device encryption and screen locks
- Uninstalling the app will delete all locally stored data

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:
- All data is stored on your device
- You can clear data by uninstalling the app
- No data is shared with us or third parties

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## No Real Trading

Important: TraderPro is a simulation app for educational purposes only. No real money or actual stock trading occurs. All transactions are simulated.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: support@traderpro.app

## Consent

By using TraderPro, you consent to this Privacy Policy.
